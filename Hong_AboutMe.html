Contact: hong1537@mydusd.org

Career Goal: I don’t know what I’m going to do when I grow up. I only know what I want to be. My name is Michael Hong, and I am a freshman at Dublin High School. When I grow up, I want to be a sound engineer. Being a sound engineer gives you a lot of creative freedom with what you want to create. You can create music through a variety of software programs, all in your own recording studio, and have fun doing it. I love listening to, and playing music, so a career where I get to do this everyday would be paradise. In order to achieve this goal, I would take as many classes on music theory as possible,learn video mixing and editing, digital signal processing, and music production. Learning the basics for sound engineering requires a lot of hard work and time, but once I become a sound engineer, I will have a lot of fun.

Strengths, Personal qualities, and Achievements: I have a couple noticable strengths and personal qualities. First of all, I have been exposed to music ever since I was a young child. I am very passionate about music, and becoming a sound engineer would allow me to pursue my dream. I am patient, well-organized, and positive, which are some important traits you would need while working with other people. Being able to cooperate and be friendly with other people is a huge part in being succesful in this field, and I believe I have these traits. I have demonstrated these traits through everyday life in the classroom, with my family, and while playing sports. A main part of a sound engineer’s job is being able to communicate with people, so I think I would be a capable sound engineer. These experiences and characteristics make up who I am, and who I will eventually become. Now, let me tell you some of my achievements. As of writing this, I have only been in high school for about three months, so I will talk about some achievements in middle school.
